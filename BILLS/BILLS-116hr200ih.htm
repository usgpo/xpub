IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

March 8, 2019

Mrs. Carolyn B. Maloney of New York (for herself and Ms. Schakowsky) submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Committee on Foreign Affairs

RESOLUTION

Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives that the Senate should give its advice and consent to the ratification of the Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination Against Women.

Resolved, That it is the sense of the House of Representatives that—

(1) the full realization of the rights of women is vital to the development and well-being of people of all nations; and

(2) the Senate should, therefore, give its advice and consent to the ratification of the Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination Against Women.
