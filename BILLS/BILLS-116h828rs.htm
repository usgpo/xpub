AN ACT

To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 25 Route 111 in Smithtown, New York, as the “Congressman Bill Carney Post Office”.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. Congressman Bill Carney Post Office.

(a) Designation.—The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 25 Route 111 in Smithtown, New York, shall be known and designated as the “Congressman Bill Carney Post Office”.

(b) References.—Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Congressman Bill Carney Post Office”.
